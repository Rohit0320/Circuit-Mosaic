Circuit Mosaic

Circuit Mosaic was a thrilling three-round electronics competition that combined strategy, design, and hands-on skills. In the first round, teams used 1500 virtual points to bid for essential circuit components in an exciting auction. The second round challenged participants to design accurate circuit diagrams using their acquired parts, testing their creativity and technical understanding. In the final round, teams brought their ideas to life by assembling and testing real circuits, showcasing their practical knowledge and problem-solving abilities.
